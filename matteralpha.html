Automation News Publishing Website

UX/UI & Branding Case Study

Designing a modern and user-friendly platform to keep up with the evolving world of home automation. Tackling problems like content overload, poor UX, and lack of visual appeal.

Challenges Identified

  1. Information Overload
  2. Poor User Experience
  3. Inadequate Visual Appeal
  4. Content Accessibility

Objectives

  1. Effectively communicate the latest in home automation
  2. Ensure a human and enjoyable user experience
  3. Design a visually appealing interface
  4. Enable design reuse if the brand pivots

Design Process

Wireframing

  • Mapped site structure and user flow
  • Focused on intuitive navigation and clear categorization

Prototyping

  • Developed interactive prototypes for testing
  • Gathered feedback from stakeholders

Visual Design

  • Modern, minimalist design aesthetic
  • Accessible, pleasing color scheme
  • Engaging use of images and videos

Conclusion

We designed a dedicated, modern platform to deliver timely and relevant home automation news. Our approach emphasized usability, visual appeal, and content clarity, helping users stay informed and engaged with the evolving tech landscape.
